About the Data:

The data from these visualizations was queried from publicly visible personal LinkedIn profiles. We filtered our search to profiles in the United States with the term "data scientist" in their profiles. We then verified that these accounts were valid and active by testing the associated corporate email addresses. From that exercise, we were able to validate 28,664 profiles in the United States. Python was then used for all of the specified analyses.
Disclaimer: The data collected is self reported and is not a representative sample of the entire United States.
