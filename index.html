The data from this report was queried from publicly visible personal LinkedIn profiles between May and July, 2020. We filtered our search to profiles in the United States with the term "data scientist" in their profiles. We then verified that these accounts were valid and active by testing the associated corporate email addresses. From that exercise, we were able to validate 28,664 profiles in the United States.

Python was used to analyze all of the specified analyses. Values for each chart were found by finding the total occurrences of each unique, specific location/job/industry within the dataset and sorting the values in descending order for the question of interest.
